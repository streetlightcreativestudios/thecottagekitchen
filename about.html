Our Beginning

A kitchen built on
love & respect

The Cottage Kitchen was born from a simple belief: that every person who sits at our table deserves to feel genuinely welcomed — regardless of what they order.

We've built a space where a bowl of kontomire stew and a plate of pan-seared salmon receive the same level of care, presentation, and professional service. Local is not lesser. It is celebrated.

Our commitment to healthy eating means we think carefully about every ingredient — sourcing fresh, cooking clean, and honouring the nutritional value of everything we prepare.
